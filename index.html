Principle: Rev.Brother Soosairaj

Montfort School, Kattur – Trichy is managed by the Christian Religious Congregation of the Montfort Brothers of St. Gabriel known for their unique service in the field of education mission around the globe. Montfort school was established in the year 2006 and now has seen unprecedented growth nurturing more than 4000 students from all walks of life. Following the CBSE syllabus the school dedicates itself to the holistic development of the human person. It is not an exaggeration to call it the real TEMPLE OF LEARNING.

What's New

  • GAJA Cyclone Relief by Montfort School, Trichy

    As the GAJA cyclone hit many parts of TamilNadu, Montfort School gathered money and essentials from our school's students and went to some places which were affected by the cyclone and helped the poor people whose homes were demolished and had no food to eat.They provided them with many essential needs such as food, water, clothes, blankets etc.


  • Introducing Creative Education

    Montfort School has reached another milestone by introducing Creative Education into her curriculum from this academic year for I to V Standards, with the collaboration from "Image Minds", Digital Audio, Video Labs are new facilities that would trigger the young creative minds.
